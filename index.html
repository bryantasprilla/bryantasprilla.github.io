About Me

I build products where the stakes are high and the details matter. My background spans regulated industries like healthcare, financial services, and global HR, where I’ve led teams turning complex systems into reliable, intuitive software, including bringing AI from early experiments into trusted, widely adopted products. I’m at my best where technology, strategy, and real-world impact meet.

Those experiences continue to influence how I work today: grounded, collaborative, and focused on building products that hold up in the real world.
