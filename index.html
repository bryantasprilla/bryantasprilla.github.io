About Me

Senior Product Leader with 8+ years driving SaaS product strategy, execution, and innovation in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) and other highly regulated industries.

I have a proven track record leading global cross-functional teams, building multi-year product roadmaps, and delivering compliance-focused solutions for financial services, healthcare, and third-party risk management. Currently based in Bulgaria, I've worked across diverse markets including the Middle East and the United States, bringing a global perspective to product development. I'm adept at aligning technology with evolving regulatory requirements, optimizing operational workflows, and achieving measurable business outcomes including ARR growth, client retention, and market expansion.

Building Discipline

2013 – 2019

My time in the Army Reserves taught me more than just discipline — it showed me how to lead under pressure, make decisions with incomplete information, and keep teams focused when things get chaotic. Those lessons became the foundation of how I approach product management today: stay calm, be decisive, and always put your team first.

🇺🇸 Verona, Wisconsin

Healthcare Tech Immersion

2017 – 2019

Epic threw me into the deep end of healthcare software. I learned that in regulated industries “move fast and break things” doesn’t work — you need to move thoughtfully and build things right the first time. Managing Level 2 IT teams across hospitals taught me that great product management is equal parts technical expertise and human empathy.

🇦🇪 Dubai, UAE

The Dubai Leap

2019 – 2022

Moving to Dubai at 25 was terrifying and exhilarating in equal measure. I learned to navigate healthcare regulations across three countries, work with executive leadership who thought differently about technology, and build products for cultures completely different from my own. It’s where I discovered that great product management is 50% empathy and 50% understanding context.

🇧🇬 Varna, Bulgaria

GRC Innovation

2022 – Present

Bulgaria brought me into the world of compliance and risk — industries where mistakes have real consequences. I championed our first LLM products when AI was still scary to most institutions, led a team through our first strategic roadmap, and learned that the best product innovation comes from deeply understanding operational pain. Managing $6M in ARR taught me that revenue growth and customer success are inseparable.
